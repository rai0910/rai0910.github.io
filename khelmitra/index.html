About Khel Mitra

Khel Mitra is a comprehensive sports platform dedicated to bringing together athletes, coaches, and sports enthusiasts. We provide a one-stop solution for all your sports needs, from training and coaching to event participation and community engagement.

Our mission is to make sports accessible to everyone and help individuals achieve their fitness and athletic goals through expert guidance, community support, and cutting-edge technology.
